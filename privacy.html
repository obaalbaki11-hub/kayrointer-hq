Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 14, 2026  ·  Kayro Interactive Inc.

1. Overview

Kayro Interactive ("Kayro", "we", "us", or "our") operates the AI workforce platform available at kayrointer.com. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

By using Kayro, you agree to the collection and use of information described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

Account information: When you sign up, we collect your name, email address, and authentication credentials. If you sign in with GitHub, we receive your GitHub profile name, email, and avatar.

Usage data: We collect information about how you use the platform — which agents you interact with, commands you run, and approximate token usage — to operate the service and enforce plan limits.

Content you provide: Messages, documents, and data you submit to AI agents are processed to generate responses. Conversation history is not permanently stored by default. However, information you explicitly save to your Brain (the company knowledge base) is stored in your account until you delete it — this is a core feature of the service.

Technical data: IP address, browser type, and session identifiers are collected for security and abuse prevention.

3. Google API & Gmail Usage

How we use your Google account

When you connect your Google account, Kayro requests permission to send emails on your behalf via the Gmail API (gmail.send scope). This allows Kayro's AI agents to draft and send outreach emails, follow-ups, and other messages directly from your Gmail address — only when you explicitly instruct them to.

  • We only send emails you have explicitly requested through the platform.
  • We do not read, index, or store the contents of your Gmail inbox.
  • We do not use your Gmail data to train AI models.
  • Access tokens are stored securely and used solely for the actions you initiate.
  • You can revoke Gmail access at any time via your Google Account permissions page.

Our use of Google APIs complies with the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

4. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide, operate, and improve the Kayro platform
  • To authenticate your identity and maintain your session
  • To enforce plan limits and usage caps
  • To send transactional emails (e.g. password resets, billing notices)
  • To detect and prevent fraud or abuse
  • To respond to your support requests

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

5. Third-Party Services

Kayro uses the following third-party services to operate the platform:

  • Firebase (Google) — authentication and user session management
  • Cloudflare — hosting, edge computing, and DDoS protection
  • Anthropic — AI model inference (Claude). Your messages are processed by Anthropic's API subject to their privacy policy.
  • Stripe — payment processing. Billing and financial data is handled directly by Stripe and subject to their privacy policy. Kayro does not store full card details.
  • Hunter.io — email address discovery (only when you use the Lead Finder feature)
  • Apollo.io — prospect data and lead enrichment (only when you use prospecting features)
  • Meta — advertising integrations (only when you use the Meta Ads connector)
  • Resend — transactional email delivery
  • Tavily — web search (only when agents perform internet searches)
  • Duffel / Amadeus — flight and travel data (only when you use the Travel Concierge booking feature)
  • Kling AI — video generation (only when you use the video ad feature on Scale plan)

Each third party is subject to their own privacy policies and data handling practices.

International transfers: Kayro is operated from the UAE. We use US-based infrastructure providers (Cloudflare, Firebase, Anthropic, Stripe). By using the platform, you acknowledge that your data may be transferred to and processed in the United States and other countries, which may have different data protection laws than your country of residence.

6. Data Retention

Account data is retained for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we remove your personal data within 30 days, except where we are required to retain it for legal or financial compliance purposes.

Usage logs and token records are retained for up to 12 months for billing and abuse prevention.

7. Security

We take security seriously. Authentication uses httpOnly session cookies to prevent client-side token theft. API keys are stored server-side and never exposed to the browser. All data in transit is encrypted via HTTPS/TLS.

No system is perfectly secure. If you discover a security issue, please contact us at dev@kayrointer.com.

8. Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data; to object to or restrict how we process it; and to data portability. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at inquire@kayrointer.com.

9. Children

Kayro is not directed at children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 16 (or under 13 in jurisdictions where that is the applicable minimum age). If you believe a minor has provided us with personal data, contact us at inquire@kayrointer.com and we will delete it promptly.

10. AI-Generated Content Disclaimer

Important: AI output is not professional advice

Kayro's AI agents — including agents that discuss legal matters, financial topics, tax questions, accounting, or investment ideas — generate output using large language models. This output is for informational and productivity purposes only.

Nothing generated by Kayro constitutes legal, financial, tax, investment, or accounting advice. AI agents are not licensed attorneys, financial advisors, CPAs, or regulated professionals. Output should not be relied upon as a substitute for qualified professional advice specific to your situation.

Kayro accepts no liability for decisions made based on AI-generated content. Always consult a qualified professional before acting on legal, financial, tax, or investment matters.

11. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) grants you additional rights:

  • Right to Know: You may request disclosure of the categories and specific pieces of personal information we have collected about you.
  • Right to Delete: You may request deletion of personal information we have collected, subject to certain exceptions.
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: We will not discriminate against you for exercising your privacy rights.
  • Do Not Sell: We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

To exercise these rights, contact us at inquire@kayrointer.com. We will respond within 45 days.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top and, for material changes, notify you by email or via a notice in the app.
